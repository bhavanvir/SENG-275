Title: Plates with 7 characters without spaces or hyphens are validated for vehicles that aren't motorcycles
Submitter: Bhavanvir Rai
email address: bhavanvir.r@gmail.com

Summary of bug: When a user enters a 7 character licence plate that doesn't include a space or hyphen for a vehicle that isn't a motorcycle, the plate is validated

Expected behaviour: The plate isn't validated

Actual behaviour: The plate is validated

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Have a vehicle that is a motorcycle
2. Enter a 7 character long licence plate, that doesn't include a space or hypen, i.e. "THISPLA"
3. See that the plate is validated, when it should be rejected


Severity: High
